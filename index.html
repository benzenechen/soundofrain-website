Sound of Rain Association is a nonprofit music organization based in the Seattle area, dedicated to promoting Chinese-American cultural exchange through live music, performances, and community engagement.

Through contemporary Chinese pop, rock, and crossover music, we aim to connect cultures, bring communities together, and create meaningful musical experiences.

Our performing band, Sound of Rain (Èõ®Èü≥‰πêÈòü), typically features 5-7 core members including vocals, electric/acoustic guitar, keyboard, bass, and drums. Beyond our core ensemble, we function as a broader music association featuring rotating musicians, guest performers, and small ensembles for community events.
